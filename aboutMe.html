Who I am:

I am a second year student at Georgia Tech majoring in Computational Media, which is a mix of Computer Science and literature, media, and communications.

What I do:

I spend most of time when not working on acadmics on wheels, roller skating around Atlanta and the surrounding area. I picked up this lovely hobby the beginning of April 2020, as the world began to get used to the effects of COVID-19.

I am well versed in Adobe InDesign, and working to be proficient in Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.

I have been lucky enough to be able to participate in Children's Healthcare of Atlanta's Miracle Marathon since my junior year of high school, allowing me to raise over $1,500 for kids who go through more than I could ever understand.

More about me:
